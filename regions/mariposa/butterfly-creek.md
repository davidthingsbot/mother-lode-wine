## Vineyards

The vineyard sits at high elevation (2,000–3,200 ft) on rocky hillside soils characteristic of this part of the Sierra Foothills.

## History

Butterfly Creek Winery is **by far the oldest** of the wineries currently operating in Mariposa County — a pioneer in this Yosemite gateway region.
