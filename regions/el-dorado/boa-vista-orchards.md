## History

The family-owned farm has been farming on Apple Hill for over a hundred years, making it one of the oldest continuously operating farms in the region.

## Notes

This is the quintessential Apple Hill experience — more farm stand than traditional winery, but wines and ciders are available for tasting.

Wine tastings are $5, and the property features fresh corndogs with soft batter alongside the extensive market selection.

For visitors seeking the authentic Apple Hill experience with wine, Boa Vista delivers.
