## History

Rucksack Cellars was built from the ground up — the owners took an idea, planted the vineyards, constructed the winery, and started a novel concept (for the time) of outdoor tasting.

The focus on outdoor wine tasting was designed so visitors could experience the beauty of El Dorado County to the fullest.

## Notes

The winery was introduced as "a new winery and a new concept" in 2017, emphasizing outdoor tasting and community building.

The Rucksack community has grown loyal, with club members receiving complimentary tastings. Non-members pay $10 for a tasting flight, with $5 waived on bottle purchase.

Watching the sunset over the vineyards from the tasting area is part of the experience.
