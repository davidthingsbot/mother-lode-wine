## History

Jim and Alanna Taff started as humble home winemakers and members of the El Dorado Home Wine Makers Association. Making their own wine was their dream.

In approximately 2008, they purchased the established Windwalker property, which had already been cultivated for wine grape production. The venerable vineyard has changed hands a few times in its 28+ growing seasons, but the Taffs have brought it to new heights.

As of 2018, they celebrated their 10th year of stewardship.

## Notes

The Taff family invites visitors to experience their lovely spacious tasting room, then relax on the shaded deck or enjoy a picnic alongside the vineyards.

The winery makes award-winning wines using sustainable practices and produces small lots that ensure quality in every bottle.

Nestled in the rolling grapeland of Fair Play near Somerset, Windwalker is described as a "long and winding road worth the drive."
