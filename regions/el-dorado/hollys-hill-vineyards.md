## Signature Wines

**Patriarche** — A Châteauneuf-du-Pape style blend that honors Tom and Holly's honeymoon love affair with Southern Rhône wines.

**Viognier** — Estate-grown at elevation, showing classic aromatic character with excellent acidity.

**Counoise** — A rare single-varietal bottling of this Southern Rhône grape. Few wineries in California produce varietal Counoise.

## Vineyards

The estate vineyards are planted **exclusively to Rhône varieties** — there are no Cabernet, Zinfandel, or other non-Rhône grapes on the property. This total dedication to Rhône varieties is rare in California.

Varieties planted include:
- Viognier
- Roussanne
- Grenache
- Mourvèdre
- Syrah
- Counoise

The vineyard was designed with input from Ron Mansfield, a recognized Rhône pioneer in California viticulture.

## History

Tom and Holly Cooper fell in love with Rhône wines when they shared a bottle of Châteauneuf-du-Pape on their honeymoon. That bottle changed their lives.

Years later, Holly decided she wanted to live on a hill — so they moved to Tom's family cattle ranch in the Sierra Nevada foothills. Holly got her hill; Tom got to fulfill his lifelong dream of planting a vineyard.

Given the couple's passion for Rhône varieties and the location's suitability for these grapes, they made the decision to go all-in on Rhône — no compromise, no other varieties.

## Notes

Holly's Hill also operates a **craft brewery** on the property, offering carefully brewed beers alongside their wines. The combination of wine tasting, craft beer, and the "majestic outdoors" creates a unique destination.

The winery describes itself as "Rugged, Refined" — capturing the combination of mountain winemaking and artisan craftsmanship.

This is one of the few wineries in California completely dedicated to Rhône varieties. Essential visiting for Rhône enthusiasts.
