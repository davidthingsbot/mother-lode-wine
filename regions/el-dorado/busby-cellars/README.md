## History

The property, a portion of the old Meyer's Ranch, includes a historic barn that was once a notable stopping point for miners changing horses en route to Placerville.

The Grahams' dream of owning a vineyard and winery was born in the early nineties when Sherrie worked as a retail wine manager and buyer. Like many enthusiasts of Sierra Foothills wines, Elliot and Sherrie first explored Napa and Sonoma before discovering the splendor of El Dorado County.

## Notes

Busby Cellars offers **on-site lodging** — unique among Fair Play wineries. Combined with nearby B&Bs and vacation rentals, this makes Fair Play a destination for extended visits rather than just day trips.

Historic downtown Placerville is just a short drive away for dining and shopping.
