## Vineyards

The estate encompasses vineyards dedicated primarily to Bordeaux varieties. The cellar holds over 3,500 cases of wine, allowing extended aging of their best wines.

## History

Hank Battjes founded Gold Hill Vineyard in 1980 after a 27-year career as an electrical engineer at GTE Sylvania. During those years, Hank developed his knowledge of home winemaking, applying engineering precision to his hobby and passion.

When a friend pointed out the potential of land near Coloma — just a mile from where gold was discovered at Sutter's Mill in 1848 — Hank found his perfect site.

In 1999, Gold Hill expanded into craft brewing, adding a seven-barrel system to produce seasonal beers. Today, daughter Bryna Dacus continues the family legacy.
