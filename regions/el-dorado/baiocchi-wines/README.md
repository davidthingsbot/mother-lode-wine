## Vineyards

Located in the Fair Play AVA, the 12-acre vineyard has the perfect terroir for Rhône varietals. The rocky, granitic hillsides with cool evening breezes nurture grapes throughout the growing season.

The vineyard is situated among the rocky, granitic hillsides of the Sierra Nevada foothills, forming the foundation to create elegant wines in the Southern Rhône tradition.

## History

Baiocchi Wines & Vineyards was developed by Greg Baiocchi in the Fair Play AVA of El Dorado County. The surname "Baiocchi" is the parent brand, created from select vineyard blocks.

## Notes

This is a family-run vineyard producing high-quality, small lot wines from estate-grown grapes. The focus on Southern Rhône traditions makes Baiocchi a unique destination for lovers of French-style wines.
