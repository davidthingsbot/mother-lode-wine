## History

Brian and Jennifer Bumgarner founded Bumgarner Winery in 2005 with a vision for high-quality wines grown locally in the El Dorado AVA.

Brian's journey to winemaking came through the wine industry — he sold wine for other producers while quietly making his own. When he eventually struck out on his own, the original vision was clear: mountain Cabernet Sauvignon.

In the early years, the vineyards, production facilities, and tasting room were all leased while the Bumgarners built their brand and grew their following. Today, they operate two tasting rooms — one in Fair Play and one in the Apple Hill area at Camino.

## Notes

Bumgarner operates with a "no gimmicks" philosophy — they focus on crafting excellent wine from El Dorado County fruit without unnecessary flash.

The dual tasting room model allows visitors to experience Bumgarner wines in two distinct settings:
- **Fair Play:** Off the beaten path, table-side service with pre-selected classics
- **Camino:** More accessible Apple Hill location

The winery offers exclusive releases and member perks through their wine club.
