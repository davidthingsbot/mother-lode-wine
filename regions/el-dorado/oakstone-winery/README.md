## History

Oakstone Winery opened in March 1997 as the culmination of 25 years of preparation by original owners John and Susan Smith. The winery operated on Slug Gulch Road until tragedy struck on July 7, 2012, when the original winery building was destroyed by a fire of undetermined origin.

The dream continued. The winery was rebuilt and continued operations.

In June 2021, the Russell family purchased Oakstone Winery, bringing a 3rd generation of owner/operators to carry on the legacy. Courtney Russell and family now operate both Oakstone and the Obscurity Cellars label.

## Notes

The second-story deck offers beautiful views of the surrounding hills and vineyards — one of the best vantage points in Fair Play.

The Obscurity Cellars label allows the winery to explore lesser-known grape varieties that wouldn't fit the main Oakstone brand. Wine adventurers should ask about current Obscurity releases.
