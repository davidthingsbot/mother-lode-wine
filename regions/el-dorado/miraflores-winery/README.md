## Vineyards

The winery overlooks 40 acres of estate vineyards from its Mediterranean-inspired tasting room. The property includes diverse microclimates suitable for a wide range of varietals.

## History

Victor and Cheryl Alvarez founded Miraflores in 2003 with a commitment to doing everything right from the start. Their attention to detail paid off quickly — before the winery had even celebrated its second anniversary, **Wine Spectator named Miraflores "a winery to watch."**

Master winemaker Marco Cappelli was instrumental in establishing Miraflores' quality standards. He trained current winemaker Fernando Abarca, ensuring continuity of the winery's distinctive style.

The results speak for themselves: Miraflores has earned **50+ wines scoring 90+ points** from Wine Spectator and Wine Enthusiast — an remarkable track record for a Sierra Foothills winery.

## Notes

The Mediterranean-inspired tasting room provides an elegant setting overlooking the estate vineyards. The winery hosts imaginative events including:
- Art in the Vineyards
- Concert series
- Chef-led cooking classes
- Locally grown farmers' markets

From June through October, their "PAIRINGS" series features chef-prepared lunches matched with award-winning wines on the covered terrace.

Marco Cappelli's sweet wines are particularly noteworthy — if you enjoy dessert wines, these are not to be missed.
