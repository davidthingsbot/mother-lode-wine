## Signature Wines

The ranch grows single-clone varietals on multiple rootstocks to increase complexity. The growers are known around the area for selling "super-clean fruit."

## Vineyards & Farm

The family vineyard sits at 2,500 feet elevation with south/southwest exposure — ideal for ripening. Beyond wine grapes, the ranch grows:

- 17 varieties of apples
- Olives
- Chestnuts
- Flowers

This diversified approach provides resilience and creates a full farmstead experience for visitors.

## History

Smokey Ridge Ranch is a diverse family operation growing all produce on-site. Ranch visits are encouraged — just call ahead.

## Notes

The winery hosts wine and cider tastings, food pairings, music, release parties, and special events throughout the year.

For those interested in the agricultural diversity of Apple Hill, Smokey Ridge offers a complete farm experience beyond just wine tasting.
