## Vineyards

The estate is primarily dedicated to the planting and cultivation of Rhône varieties. Located at 1,300 feet elevation in the Greater El Dorado area, the property benefits from warm days and cool nights.

The vineyard sits just one mile from the gold discovery site at Sutter's Mill in Coloma, connecting it directly to California's Gold Rush heritage.

## History

David Girard Vineyards was established in 1994, contributing to the ongoing revival of winemaking in El Dorado County. The founders recognized the region's potential for Rhône varieties and dedicated the estate to these grapes.

The proximity to Sutter's Mill — where James Marshall discovered gold in 1848 — adds historical significance to every visit.

## Notes

The property serves as a popular Tuscan Italian-style wedding and event venue. The combination of history, culture, and exceptional Rhône wines makes this a complete destination.

The elevated setting provides views of the surrounding foothills, creating an atmospheric tasting experience.
