## Signature Wines

**Estate Pinot Noir** — Grown creekside with cool air flow from the mountains and well-drained, chalky soils. The 3,000-foot elevation creates a unique microclimate for this notoriously difficult variety.

## Vineyards

Located at 3,000-foot elevation — among the highest in El Dorado County — Findleton has a unique vineyard site that provides a distinctive microclimate. The estate Pinot Noir vineyard sits creekside with cool mountain air flow.

## History

Voted "Best Kept Secret in El Dorado County," Findleton Estate Winery is a family operation committed to high-quality, handcrafted wines at affordable prices.

## Notes

The uniquely designed tasting room features the "wave bar" and creative decor. The French-inspired "Rhône Room" hosts private winemaker dinners.

The winery offers wine education experiences and classes — check the website for current programming.

All wine labels feature artwork by local favorite Pamela Findleton, creating a visual identity for the brand.
