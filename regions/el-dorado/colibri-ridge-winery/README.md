## Vineyards

The vineyard and winery are located high on a ridgetop within the Fair Play AVA, on the western slope of the Sierra Nevada Foothills in the southern part of El Dorado County.

The 21+ acre property has a 3,600 sq ft fully insulated winery building with 20-foot ceiling clearance.

## History

Colibri Ridge Winery & Vineyard is a small family-owned winery. The name "Colibri" is Spanish for hummingbird — a nod to the wildlife that frequents the ridgetop property.

## Notes

The winery has operated at up to 2,000 cases per year but has capacity to expand to 4,500 cases annually.

**Directions:** From the store at Mt. Aukum Rd & Fair Play Rd, proceed south on Fair Play Rd to the 2nd Perry Creek Rd exit, left on Perry Creek Rd, right on Slug Gulch Rd, left on Vineyard View Rd, left on Gray Rock Rd. Follow signs to Colibri Ridge Winery.
