## History

Charles B. Mitchell "wasted no time, blazing a trail" when he purchased the largest of Fair Play's original wineries to reawaken the area. Like the Gold Rush pioneers who took chances 100 years before him, Mitchell bet on Fair Play.

He became known as the self-proclaimed **"Emperor of Fair Play"** — a title that reflected his outsized personality and role in developing the region's wine identity.

Mitchell passed in 2025, but his legacy continues. The community celebrated his life in a toast at Fireman's Hall in Somerset.
