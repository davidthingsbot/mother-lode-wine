## Signature Wines

**Estate Syrah** — Dry, rich, and full-bodied with blackberry, currant, and spice. Open-fermented, hand-punched, and aged for 3 years in mostly neutral European oak barrels. Sourced from the estate vineyard at 2,400 feet.

## Vineyards

The estate vineyard sits at 2,400 feet elevation in the Fair Play AVA. The high altitude and granitic soils create fruit with excellent structure for aging.

## Winemaking Philosophy

Cantiga uses a natural, **non-malolactic (non-ML) method** with minimal processing and minimal sulfites. This approach is seldom seen today and produces wines that:
- Align exceptionally well with food
- Benefit from long periods of cellaring
- Age gracefully over time

Winemaker Rich Rorden strives to recapture the art of making classic-style wines — a throwback to traditional winemaking before modern interventions.

## History

Cantiga Wineworks is a boutique, family-run winery in Fair Play. The name "Cantiga" refers to a type of medieval Portuguese song — fitting for a winery dedicated to old-world winemaking methods.

## Notes

The tasting room sits on a stunning knoll top overlooking the estate vineyards. Visitors can enjoy food and wine pairing flights while wandering through the gardens.

For those interested in traditional winemaking techniques, Cantiga offers a unique perspective on what wine can be without modern processing.
