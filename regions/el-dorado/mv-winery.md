## Signature Wines

**Estate Syrah** — Plush and elegant, showcasing Fair Play's potential for Rhône varieties.

**Pinotage** — A South African cross of Pinot Noir and Cinsault, rarely found in California.

**Lagrein** — An obscure Italian variety from Alto Adige, almost never planted in California. MV is one of the few US producers.

## Vineyards

The estate vineyard covers 11 acres planted to:
- Syrah
- Petite Sirah
- Cabernet Sauvignon
- Zinfandel
- Pinotage
- Lagrein

The focus on rare varieties like Pinotage and Lagrein makes MV's vineyard one of the most unusual in the Sierra Foothills.

## History

MV Winery has chosen a path of high quality, low production wines — bottles so rich and complex that you would remember drinking them a week, month, or even a year later.

The winery has received multiple national and international awards, validating their quality-over-quantity philosophy.

## Notes

If you're seeking something truly different, MV Winery delivers with varieties like Pinotage and Lagrein that most California wine drinkers have never encountered. The plush, elegant red wines offer memorable tasting experiences.
