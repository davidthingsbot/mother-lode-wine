## History

Andis Wines offers a unique Amador experience with spacious grounds, a modern tasting room, bocce ball court, and welcoming staff. The new event center hosts private parties, weddings, business meetings, and special events.

## Notes

Gourmet cheeses, charcuterie, and wine-friendly snacks available. Ask about the wine on tap and refillable carafe program — a sustainable approach to wine enjoyment.

The expansive vineyard views and modern facilities make Andis a standout destination.
