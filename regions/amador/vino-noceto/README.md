## Signature Wines

**Sangiovese** — Vino Noceto has been making "California's Best Sangiovese" for over three decades. With eight different Sangioveses available, this is the definitive destination for lovers of this Tuscan grape.

**Frivolo** — A playfully sparkling Moscato.

## History

The name "Noceto" means walnut grove in Italian — walnuts were a staple crop on this farm before vines. Additionally, Noceto, Italy (near Parma) is the sister city of owner Suzy's native town of Walnut Creek.

## Notes

If you love Sangiovese, this is the single most important stop in Amador County. With eight different expressions, visitors can explore the full range of what this varietal offers in Sierra Foothills terroir.
