## History

Convergence Vineyards is set among beautiful blue-oak woodland, traversed by rolling hills and creeks. The sustainable vineyard and highly-acclaimed winery are family-owned and operated.

## Notes

"Come see us at our tasting room to learn about our process, meet our family, and share in our passion for winemaking!"

### Hidden Gem Within a Hidden Gem
Founded in 2002, producing small-lot award-winning wines by hand. The winery sits on a knoll overlooking the **convergence of three creeks** — hence the name.

**Awards:** Wines consistently win at California wine competitions — multiple Double Gold, Best in Class, and Best of Show awards.

**Hosts Steve and Jamie Concannon** "will make you feel welcome and teach you a lot about wine and food pairing." Try **"Mister Bubbles"** — "not too dry, not too sweet, and goes down very easily."

Part of California's most historic wine region, home to the oldest living grapevines in North America.
