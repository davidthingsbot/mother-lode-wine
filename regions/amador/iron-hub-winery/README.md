## History

Father-son winemakers Tom and Spencer Jones handcraft each wine and spirit, tending to every vintage from vine to bottle.

## Notes

Experience elegant wines, smooth spirits, and spectacular Sierra and vineyard views from the modern tasting room, spacious patio, or covered pavilion.

Seated, guided wine tastings are available — reservations recommended. Gourmet cheeses, charcuterie, and wine-friendly snacks available for purchase.

The patio is "perfect for your picnic."
