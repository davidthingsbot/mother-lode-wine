## Winemaking Philosophy

The End of Nowhere was founded on a simple belief: **Great California wine doesn't need chemical additives, new oak, or a big price tag to be delicious.**

## History

Founding winemaker Chris Walsh didn't have his epiphany in a vineyard — he had it in a bar. After working as a sommelier in New York City, Chris was ready to trade long nights for a new career.

He realized his childhood home in Amador County was the perfect place. Thanks to years behind the bar, Chris was well-versed in California wine, and as a local he deeply understood the potential of Amador's rugged, high-elevation terroir.

## Notes

This is natural winemaking with a clear point of view — proving that exceptional wine can come without intervention or inflated prices.
