## History

Val du Vino is the **only working winery on Main Street in Murphys**, housed in a historical Gold Country barn at the west end of town.

Mark Hoover, owner and winemaker, is committed to producing authentic Rhône and Spanish varietals from estate grapes and locally sourced fruit.

## Notes

Indoor tastings or outdoor patio overlooking the scenic vineyard. The historic barn setting adds atmosphere to the experience.
