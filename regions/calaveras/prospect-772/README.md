## History

Nestled on a stunning 25-acre property, the winery offers a serene setting for wine lovers to relax and explore.

## Notes

No reservations required. The winery hosts private events, weddings, and live music, creating a vibrant atmosphere.

### Rhône-Style Focus
Dedicated to crafting the best **Rhône-style wines** at the Appaloosa Vineyard. Just under 2 hours from the Bay Area — about 10 minutes west of Angels Camp.

**Favorites:** Try **Stepping Stones** (Grenache Blanc) and **The Brawler** (Syrah-based red).

**Reviewer tip:** The owner-winemaker is welcoming — even opened early for one visitor who arrived before official hours. "Well worth the jog in your trip."

The winery sits on beautiful countryside — go for the views and stay for the wine.
