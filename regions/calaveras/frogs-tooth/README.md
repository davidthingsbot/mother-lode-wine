## History

Since 1999, Frog's Tooth Vineyards and Winery has been growing, crafting, pouring, and sharing hand-crafted small-batch wines from the Sierra Foothills.

What they don't grow on their beautiful 10-acre vineyard in Copperopolis, they source from other local growers who share their passion.

## Notes

A quarter-century of dedicated small-batch winemaking has built Frog's Tooth's reputation in Calaveras County.
