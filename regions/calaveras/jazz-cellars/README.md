## Notes

"Where great wines, music and good times come together" — the Jazz Cellars philosophy combines award-winning wine with a festive atmosphere.

### Over 100 Awards
Accumulated **well over 100 awards** in a short period — without a dedicated vineyard! They search and find the "right terroir" across California to produce exceptional wines. No small feat.

**Live music:** Music always playing, with **live performances every Saturday from late May through October**.

**Unique approach:** Single varietal, single vineyard wines sourced from the highest quality vineyards statewide. Dog-friendly, laid-back setting.

**Favorites to try:** The Pinot Noir gets special mentions — reviewers describe it as "Four Aces" quality.
