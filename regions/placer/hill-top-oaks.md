## Vineyards

The vines catch the prevailing delta breeze during the day and cooler evening winds drifting off the Sierra Nevadas. The rich organic soil and gentle winds nourish the vines, making these wines among Loomis' best.

## Winemaking Philosophy

Estate-grown wines handcrafted and barrel-aged on-site. No flavor-manipulating additives, extracts, or industry tricks. Just sun-ripened grapes, yeast, and a smidgen of sulfite for protection.

## History

Named by Jean Davis in **1965**, the property was formerly home to Shorthorn cattle. Today, the vines take advantage of the fertile organic soil where the Shorthorns once roamed.
