## History

Twin Rocks Estate Winery lies along the Placer Wine Trail in the Sierra Foothills AVA — a region with a strong winemaking heritage beginning in the early days of the California Gold Rush.

European winemakers immigrated here alongside gold seekers, eager to cultivate vineyards in the challenging yet robust terroir.

## Notes

The winery honors this Gold Rush winemaking tradition while creating contemporary expressions of Sierra Foothills terroir.
