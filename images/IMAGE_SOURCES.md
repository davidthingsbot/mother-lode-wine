## Additional Winery Photos

*To be added as more wineries are documented.*

Winery photos should be:
1. **Your own photos** from visits (preferred)
2. **Press images** from winery websites (with permission)
3. **CC-licensed images** from Wikimedia Commons
4. **Unsplash images** of generic vineyards/wine

**Do NOT use:**
- Google Images scraped results
- Social media photos without permission
- Copyrighted marketing materials

## Adding New Images

When adding images, document them here:

```markdown
### folder/filename.jpg
- **Source:** URL or "personal photo"
- **Photographer:** Name
- **License:** CC-BY-SA 4.0 / Unsplash / Personal
- **Date:** When taken/downloaded
```
